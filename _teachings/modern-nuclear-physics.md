## Course Overview

Students will gain a deep understanding of the structure and dynamics of the atomic nucleus. The course covers both theoretical frameworks and experimental techniques used in modern laboratories.

## Prerequisites

- Quantum Mechanics I
- Electromagnetism I

## Textbooks

- "Introductory Nuclear Physics" by Kenneth S. Krane
- "Nuclear Physics in a Nutshell" by Carlos A. Bertulani
